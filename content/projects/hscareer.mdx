## Pros

- **Hands on Experience:** Made business model, identified users w/MOM test, solved problem
- **Networking + Presenting:** Created and pitched decks, pitching to local tutoring centers. Met founders in the app and social network space.
- **MVP:** Developed MVP. Learned a lot about app dev + basic database schematics


## Cons

- **Team:** Solo project.
- **Scalability:** Did not put resources into this, just free tier of firebase
- **Moat:** ex: data, distribution, design weak.
